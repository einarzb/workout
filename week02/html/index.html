The Best Pancake Recipe

Introduction

This is the best recipe for pancakes!

Ingredients

  1. 2 eggs
  2. 500g plain flour
  3. 1 tbsp sunflower oil
  4. pinch salt
  5. 300ml semi-skimmed milk

Directions

Blend

In large bowl, whisk flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Add milk, butter and egg;
stir until flour is moistened.

Thickening the Blend

Heat 12-inch nonstick skillet or griddle over medium heat until drop of water sizzles;
brush lightly with oil. In batches, scoop batter by scant 1/4-cupfuls into skillet,
spreading to 3 1/2 inches each. Cook 2 to 3 minutes or until bubbly and edges are dry.

Cooking First Side

With wide spatula, turn; cook 2 minutes more or until golden.
